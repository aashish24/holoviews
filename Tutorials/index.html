The best way to learn what Holoviews can do and how to use it is to study the HoloViews tutorials:

  • Homepage: Images to go on the HoloViews home page.
  • Showcase: What HoloViews can do for you and your data.
  • Introduction: How to use HoloViews – basic concepts and getting started.
  • HoloMap:: Introduction to the data structure that flexibly holds all your data ready for selecting, sampling, slicing, viewing, and combining.
  • Advanced HoloMap: Advanced applications of HoloMap for experienced users. (Coming soon!)
  • Components: Examples of all the major HoloViews components and their usage.

For more detailed information on particular components, please refer to the Reference Manual: or the online help (e.g. type Matrix? in an IPython Notebook cell).
